We use cookies to enhance your experience on our website. This page allows you to manage your cookie preferences.

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us analyze website traffic and provide personalized experiences.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to remember your preferences, enhance site performance, and personalize content. Some cookies are essential for website functionality.

3. Managing Cookies

You can control the use of cookies through your browser settings. You can disable cookies, but this may affect certain features of our website.

4. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the basic operation of the website.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us analyze website traffic and user behavior.
  • Functional Cookies: Allow us to personalize your experience on our website.

5. Consent

By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies. You can withdraw your consent at any time by adjusting your browser settings.
