At Casual-Bazaar, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not satisfied, we offer a straightforward return process outlined below.

1. Return Eligibility

You can return items within 30 days of receiving your order, provided that the items are unused, undamaged, and in their original packaging. Some items, such as perishable goods or personalized products, may not be eligible for return.

2. How to Initiate a Return

If you would like to return an item, please follow these steps:

  • Contact our customer service team at support@casual.bazarr.com to request a return authorization.
  • Provide your order number, the item(s) you wish to return, and the reason for the return.
  • Once your return is approved, we will send you a return shipping label.

3. Return Shipping

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item is defective or damaged. We will provide a prepaid return label for defective or incorrect items.

4. Refunds and Exchanges

Once we receive your returned item, we will inspect it and process your refund or exchange. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method within 7-10 business days. If you prefer an exchange, we will ship the replacement item once the return is processed.

5. Non-Returnable Items

The following items are not eligible for return:

  • Gift cards
  • Personalized or custom products
  • Perishable goods
  • Intimate or sanitary products

6. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact our customer service team within 7 days of receiving the product. We will provide you with a prepaid return label and issue a full refund or replacement item.
